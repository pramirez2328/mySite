Pedro Ramirez turned his programming hobby into a professional career. He graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems with a concentration in web development. The love he has for programming and learning was recognized when he was chosen as class valedictorian. In addition to his bachelor’s degree, he has earned several tech certifications and attended an intense 10-week full-stack web development Bootcamp. His expertise in solving problems comes from working for more than a decade as a project manager. During this time, he gained experience working with teams, dealing with and meeting deadlines, as well as, working under pressure.

Solving logistical problems by implementing meaningful strategies has been consistently part of his professional life. Now he has transferred his problem-solving skills into the tech world by creating real projects using the latest technologies. He believes learning is a never-ending journey, so he is planning on obtaining his master’s in Computer Science soon.

In his free time, he enjoys doing community work with his church.
