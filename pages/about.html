I have had the privilege of transforming my passion for programming into a deeply fulfilling professional journey. Holding a bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems with a specialization in web development, I take immense pride in the path I've carved. My dedication to programming and continuous growth culminated in the honor of being selected as the class valedictorian.

In addition to my formal education, I've actively pursued diverse tech certifications and seized the exhilarating opportunity to attend an intensive 10-week full-stack web development boot camp. This journey has been enriched by over a decade of experience as a project manager. Through this role, I finely honed essential skills like problem-solving, teamwork, and meeting deadlines even amidst challenging circumstances. Addressing logistical hurdles and finding effective solutions has been a consistent theme throughout my professional tenure.

Leveraging my problem-solving skills seamlessly into the tech realm, I've successfully brought meaningful projects to fruition using cutting-edge technologies. A significant chapter of this journey was my role as a front-end engineer at Snap Inc., where I played a vital part in integrating the Shopping Suite SDK into a range of merchant websites. This was a pivotal initiative aimed at uniting Snap’s AR and AI technology into a cohesive service, addressing major challenges within the retail industry and positively transforming the shopping experience for consumers.

During my two-year tenure in this position, I shouldered the responsibility of constructing, testing, and maintaining a particular demo website. This platform effectively showcased the diverse features of the SDK, including Fit Finder, clothing try-on, eyewear try-on, and a 3D viewer. The goal was to provide a comprehensive and engaging demonstration of the SDK's capabilities to potential clients and stakeholders.

My belief in lifelong learning has led me to pursue further education as a part-time student, currently undertaking a Master's degree in Software Development at the esteemed University of Boston. Beyond the realm of technology, I find immense joy in giving back to the community through active involvement with my church. This involvement serves as a source of relaxation and allows me to make a positive contribution to the world around me.
